## How I Work

- **Product first, code second.** i prototype to sharpen judgment, not to replace engineers. Building small things myself makes me a better partner to the teams that build big things.
- **AI-fluent by practice, not by certificate.** i use these tools daily, know where they break, and know what that means for what a roadmap should and shouldn't promise.
- **Decisions over documentation.** every project here states the problem, the bet, and what i cut. That discipline is the same one i bring to product teams.
